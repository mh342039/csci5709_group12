Questions about Registration

Question : Are there any special requirements for Registration ?
Answer: No, there are no special requirements except being a student at Dalhousie.

Question : Do any email work for registration ?
Answer : Only Dal emails work for registration.

Question : What data is collected when I register ?
Answer : The email, name and password are the only required data for registration.

 Questions about Signing in

Question : How do I sign in with gmail account ?
Answer: No, you cannot sign in with gmail account. You need a dal email.

Question : Where can I reset my password ?
Answer : You can use the forgot password for resetting password.

Question : Is there any other way of signing in except dal email ?
Answer : No.

Questions about the program

Question : What is the aim of the program ?
Answer: The main aim of the program is to help the students coming to Dalhousie for education in all ways possible.

Question : Who are the mentors ?
Answer : The students who joined the program, have been in the program for a while, and want to help future students are the part of this.

Question : What are my benefits ?
Answer : The students who are part of this program get a mentor, a place to meet new people and get help.

 Questions about the mentors

Question : Are the mentors helpful ?
Answer: The mentors help the students in any way possible.

Question : What are the mentors here for ?
Answer : The mentors help the upcoming students with the problems they faced when they came here and be there for academic help too.

Question : How can I become a mentor
Answer : You need to join the program and be affiliated with it for few months. Then you can become a mentor.
